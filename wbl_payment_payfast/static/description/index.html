Payfast Payment Acquirer

The "Payfast Payment Gateway" app for Odoo facilitates the integration of the Payfast payment provider into your Odoo website. By entering the necessary merchant key and configuration details, administrators can easily activate Payfast, providing customers with a secure and reliable payment option during the checkout process. This payment method is also prominently displayed on customer invoices. Admins can create customer invoices within the backend, selecting Payfast as the preferred payment method and generating a unique payment link. Customers can utilise this link to complete their payments and streamline order confirmation.
